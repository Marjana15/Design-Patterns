# Observer Pattern

In this scenario, a News Agency acts as a central hub for news distribution, serving two initial subscribers, Subscriber 1 and Subscriber 2. News updates, including new legislation and the weekly weather forecast, are promptly conveyed to both subscribers. Later, Subscriber 1 opts out, no longer receiving updates.
